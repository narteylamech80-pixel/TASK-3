 📊 Interactive Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **Interactive Sales Dashboard** developed using **Power BI Desktop**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profit trends, product distribution, and customer purchasing behavior across different time periods.

It is designed to support **data-driven decision-making** by offering clear visual insights into key business metrics.

---

 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Track profit and profitability percentage
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Monitor monthly and daily sales trends
* Evaluate sales channels and payment methods

---

 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following key fields:

* **Quantity** – Number of items sold
* **Selling Price** – Price per unit
* **Discount %** – Applied discount
* **Total Selling Value** – Final revenue after discount
* **Product & Category** – Item classification
* **Sales Type** – Direct or Online
* **Payment Mode** – Cash or Online
* **Date (Year, Month, Day)** – Time-based analysis

---

 🧮 Key Metrics

* **Total Sales:** 63.28K
* **Quantity Sold:** 778
* **Total Profit:** $4.01K
* **Profit Percentage:** 6.76%

---

 📈 Dashboard Features

 🔹 Filters (Slicers)

* Year (2024, 2025)
* Month (January – December)
* Sales Type
* Payment Mode
  
🔹 Visualizations

 **Sales & Profit by Month** (Trend analysis)
 **Daily Sales** (Short-term performance)
 **Sales by Product** (Top-performing products)
 **Sales by Category** (Category comparison)
 **Sales by Type** (Direct vs Online)
  **Sales by Payment Mode** (Cash vs Online)

---

 📊 Key Insights

 Sales performance varies across months, ranging between **5K and 9K**
 A few products contribute significantly to total revenue
 Profit margin is moderate at **6.76%**
 Sales channels (Direct vs Online) show balanced contributions
 Payment modes (Cash and Online) are nearly evenly distributed

---

 💡 Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue
* Introduce promotions during low-sales months
* Optimize pricing and discount strategies
* Enhance online sales channels for scalability

---

 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard creation and visualization
* **Data Modeling** – Relationships and calculated measures
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations (e.g., Total Selling Value)

---

 📌 Formula Used

**Total Selling Value Calculation:**

[
\text{Total Selling Value} = \text{Quantity} \times \text{Selling Price} \times (1 - \text{Discount %})
]
